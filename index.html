Mission

Modernizing the Caribbean’s digital backbone

RYX exists to close the gap between subsea cables, data centers, and the cloud. We unify fragmented networks, reduce latency, and enable sovereign, high‑performance access to global platforms—so Caribbean nations can build, scale, and own their digital future.

RYX Platform

The core layers of the Caribbean Grid

RYX provides the foundational infrastructure needed to modernize connectivity across the Caribbean. Our platform is built around three core layers that improve performance, resilience, and regional autonomy.

⚡
Edge Performance Layer
Low‑latency execution and content delivery across the region, enabling faster apps, APIs, and digital services.
🛡️
Security & Resilience Layer
Built‑in protection against outages, attacks, and regional fragility—designed for the Caribbean’s unique risks.
🌐
Regional Routing Fabric
A unified interconnect layer that keeps traffic local when possible and optimally routed when necessary.
Impact

Why this matters now

The Caribbean is too important—and too vulnerable—to rely on distant infrastructure paths. RYX is designed to make the region faster, more resilient, and more sovereign by default.

• Reduce latency for users, businesses, and governments across the region.
• Keep more traffic and data flows in‑region where policy and sovereignty require it.
• Improve resilience against cable cuts, outages, and extreme weather events.
• Give local operators and governments more control over how traffic is routed.
• Enable new digital services that assume low‑latency, high‑reliability connectivity.
• Attract investment and innovation by modernizing the region’s digital foundation.
Founder
