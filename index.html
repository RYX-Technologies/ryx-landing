Mission

Modernizing the Caribbean's digital future

The Caribbean region deserves world-class digital infrastructure. RYX is building the foundation that enables Caribbean nations, businesses, and communities to access, build, and own their digital future — on their own terms, at global standards.

Technology

Cloud-native infrastructure

RYX is built on a multi-cloud architecture leveraging the world's leading cloud platforms to deliver high-performance, resilient digital infrastructure across the Caribbean basin.

Amazon Web Services
Cloud compute · Content delivery · DNS · Monitoring
Google Cloud Platform
Cloud compute · Content delivery · DNS · Network services
Microsoft Azure
Cloud compute · Content delivery · DNS · Traffic management

Our approach ensures redundancy, regional flexibility, and resilience — eliminating single points of failure across Caribbean markets.

Impact

Why this matters now

The Caribbean is too important — and too vulnerable — to rely on the status quo. RYX is designed to make the region faster, more resilient, and more sovereign by default.

•Improve digital performance for users, businesses, and governments across the region.
•Strengthen digital sovereignty — giving Caribbean nations more control over their own infrastructure.
•Build resilience against outages, extreme weather events, and regional disruptions.
•Enable new digital services that Caribbean communities and economies need to grow.
•Close the digital access gap between the Caribbean and the rest of the world.
•Attract investment and innovation by giving the region a world-class digital foundation.
